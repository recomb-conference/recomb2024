How to Register

Registration for RECOMB 2024 and Satellite Conferences will take place through our registration website. You may pay via credit card or SWIFT. Your registration will be complete only when the payment is successful and the system displays a success message.

Registration for RECOMB 2024 is now open. Click here to register!

The registration fees for RECOMB 2024 include attendance at all talks and panels, breakfast on all days, one gala dinner and refreshments during coffee breaks and poster sessions. Fees are in USD.

The early Registration Deadline is March 15, 2024. After this deadline, all prices will be increased by $100. Satellite registrants may attend any satellite of their choice at each session. However, we investigate the main satellite of choice of each attendant in the registration form to help us assign a suitably-sized conference room to each satellite conference.

Cancellations and other changes may be made through the registration website until April 19, 2024 at 11:59PM EDT. To cancel or make a change after that date, please email conferences-www@mit.edu. Refunds for cancellations received in writing by April 7, 2024 at 11:59PM EDT will be fully granted for a $50 processing fee.

!! No refunds will be granted after April 7, 2024 !!

What's included:

  • Attendance to all talks in the registered conference (main RECOMB and/or satellites)
  • Gala dinner (main RECOMB only)
  • Breakfast on all days
  • Lunch on Sunday (satellites only)
  • Refreshments during coffee breaks and poster sessions
