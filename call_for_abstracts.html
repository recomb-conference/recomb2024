Call for Posters

Posters

RECOMB 2024 invites the submission of abstracts for the poster track. Posters can also be simultaneously submitted for display at one of the Satellite meetings' poster sessions. If posters are accepted at both RECOMB and one of the satellite meetings, presenters are welcome to display their posters at both meetings.

Please submit a 250-word abstract of your poster that describes its primary results. Your submission should also include the author(s), their affiliations, the presenting author, the e-mail address of at least the contact author and the poster title as requested by the submission system. The abstract should be plain text and should not contain any figures.

Please visit the EasyChair system to submit your poster abstract. The submission system will close on February 4th, 2024. Acceptance notifications will be sent out on February 19th, 2024.

For further questions, please contact the Posters Chair, Joshua Welch.

EasyChair link for poster submission: https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=recomb2024.

Any questions? Email organizers at recomb2024@mit.edu
